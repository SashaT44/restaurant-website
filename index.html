About

The Spot Mouette

Named after the French word for seagull.

Mouette is more than a café — it’s a gentle idea, inspired by the seagull, of a space that feels pleasant, comfortable, and effortlessly elegant.

A haven for contemporary parents to relax, recharge, and enjoy time with their children.

A space that meets the child's world halfway — calm, creative, and welcoming.
